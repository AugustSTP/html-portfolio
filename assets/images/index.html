The Best Movies According to August

My top movies

Amelie

A quirky French woman decides to change the lives of those around her for the better.

The Time That Remains

A semi-autobiographical drama about the creation of Israel from the perspective of a Palestinian Christian family.

Mr Nobody

In a world where people stop aging at 25, a 117 year old man looks back trying to remember his past and the choices he made.

Interstellar

A team of astronauts travel through a wormhole to find a new home for humanity among the stars.

As Above So Below

Archaeologists searching for treasure in the catacombs of Paris encounter sinister forces and secrets buried beneath the city.

The Matador's Mistress

The lover of a famous but flawed matador reflects on their tumultuous affair and its tragic consequences.

The Landlord
